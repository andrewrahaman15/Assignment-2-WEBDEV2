Leading Lines

In my opinion, this picture is a perfect representation of the compositional device known as leading lines. The wooden framing follows the same sequence throughout, with each wooden divider being of the same width and height and are all equally spaced out. A shutter speed of 1/50 was used to take this shot along with an ISO of 100 and an aperture of f-stop 3.5. I feel this shot was very successful in particular as there are multiple examples of leading lines displayed within both the couches and framing. The relatively fast shutter speed creates an ideal amount of exposure for the shot showing depth in the shot, particularly at the curve in the image. The ISO of 100 along with the aperture of f3.5 gives the framing more detail showing features such as shadows along the curves in the shot.
