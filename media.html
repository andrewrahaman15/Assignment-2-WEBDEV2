My Media Work

Welcome to the media section of this portfolio. This section will display my media collection to date. These video projects were created as part of media and audio modules within the course..

I have been trained in the use of both the audio editing software Audacity and the video editing software Adobe Premeire Pro CC. These softwares were used in the creation of two video assignments, These assignments can be viewed below along with a short description of each project.

Can it Be Done Tomorrow?

This video montage was created as part of the module, StoryTelling and Narrative. The underlining theme to this video is the negative implications of procrastination. The purpose of this video is to show procrastination as a process. The subjects dismay to the fact his assignment is due is contrasted with the activities he undertakes rather than completing the important task.

Nelson Mandela:The Man Who Freed A Nation

This video was created as a historical piece exploring the life of Nelson Mandela, from his release to his presidency. As a worldwide icon and personal hero of mine, my aim was to show how Mandela ultimately changed South Africa for the better, through ending apartheid and uniting a nation. This short video was created using the software, IMovie along with resources such as Google Images and Youtube for resources.
