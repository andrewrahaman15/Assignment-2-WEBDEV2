Studio Headshot

This headshot was taken for purposes of examination. The aim of this headshot was to demonstrate an 8:1 ratio in terms of key, fill and backlighting. Students were given a total of five minutes each in order to complete this practical exam. In this time students were expected to position each light correctly, get a meter lighty reading using the handheld meter along with the trigger placed on the DSLR.
